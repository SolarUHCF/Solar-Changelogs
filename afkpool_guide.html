Overview
🏊
The Zone
There's a special AFK zone on the server. Enter it and stand still to earn free rewards over time — no fighting needed.
⏱️
Timer
Your personal timer counts up every second you're inside the zone. Rewards unlock automatically at certain milestones.
🔄
Repeatable
Once you've claimed all rewards, your timer resets and you can earn everything again from the beginning.
🚪
Leave = Reset
If you leave the zone, your timer resets back to zero. Stay inside to keep your progress.
// how it works
1
Find the AFK Pool zone on the server and walk into it. You'll see a message confirming you entered.
2
Stay inside. Your timer counts up every second. Use /afkpool at any time to check your progress.
3
Rewards are given automatically when you hit the required time — no clicking needed.
4
After all rewards are claimed your timer resets and you can start earning again immediately.

🕐
Rewards and the timer are only active between 14:00 and 22:00. Outside these hours you can still stand in the zone, but your timer will be paused and no rewards will be given.
🌍
All times are German time (CET/CEST) — this applies to players from Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Switzerland, France, and all other countries in the same time zone.
03
// what you earn
Rewards
🎁
There are 2 reward tiers. Each one is given automatically when your timer reaches the required time. After the second reward your timer resets and you can earn both again.
1
15 Minutes — First reward tier
Stay in the zone for 15 minutes of active time to unlock this.
2
30 Minutes — Second reward tier
Reach 30 minutes total to claim the second and final reward of the cycle.
ℹ️
Only time spent inside the zone during 14:00–22:00 counts. Paused time (outside these hours) does not advance your timer.
