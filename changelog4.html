Sumo Event
PvP · Platform Combat

Join by clicking [Click to Join] in chat or by typing /sumoevent join. All participants get teleported onto a Sumo Platform — your inventory is saved and fully returned to you after the event. No items are available during the fight. The last player standing on the platform wins the event and receives the configured rewards.

Spleef Event
Minigame · Snowblock Arena

Join by clicking [Click to Join] in chat or by typing /spleef join. All players are teleported to a snow platform and given an Efficiency X Diamond Shovel. After a 10-second free-roam phase (no hitting, no breaking), the round starts. Dig the snow beneath your opponents to make them fall. Anyone who drops below the arena floor is eliminated. The last player remaining on the platform wins. Your inventory is saved and returned after the event.

Join Command/spleef join
Win ConditionLast Standing
ToolDiamond Shovel Eff. X
InventorySaved & Restored

MineSweeper Event
Minigame · Mine Field Race

Join by clicking [Click to Join] in chat or by typing /minesweeper join. All participants are teleported to the start of a mine field scattered with hidden pressure plates. After a 10-second countdown, race across the field to reach the finish line on the other side. Step on a pressure plate and you're instantly eliminated — jump over them to survive. The first player to cross the finish line wins. Your inventory is saved and returned after the event.

Join Command/minesweeper join
Win ConditionCross Finish Line
Eliminated ByPressure Plates
InventorySaved & Restored
