02
// how to get coins
Earning Coins
⚔️
Kill Reward
Every player kill earns you 10 Coins. Simple as that.
🏪
Auction House
Sell your items to other players on the /ah. Set your own price and duration.
🎰
Gamble
Risk your coins on the slot machine for a chance at 3x your bet.
🎟️
Lottery
Buy up to 3 tickets per day. Winner takes the entire pot at 18:00.
🎯
Bounties
Claim the bounty on a player by killing them. The coins land in your balance instantly.
⚠️
Kill Boost Protection: Kill boosting is detected automatically. You'll be banned from earning coins for 3 hours (strike 1–2) or 24 hours (strike 3), with a server-wide announcement.
03
// player market
Auction House
📦
Listing Fee
Every listing costs a flat 10 Coins fee. This is non-refundable, even if you cancel early.
📋
Max Listings
You can have up to 5 active listings at a time. Cancel one to free up a slot.
⏱️
Duration
Set the listing duration from 1 to 24 hours. Default is 12h. Expired items are returned automatically.
// how to sell
1
Hold the item you want to sell in your hand and type /ah sell <price>
The full stack in your hand will be listed, not just one item.
2
Duration GUI opens. Left-click the clock to add an hour, right-click to remove. Min 1h, max 24h, default 12h.
3
Click the green Confirm button to list. The 10 Coin fee is deducted and your item is removed from your hand.
4
When someone buys your item you get a private notification. Go to /ah → Your Listings and double-click the sold item to claim your coins.
// how to buy
1
Open /ah and click Browse Auction House to see all active listings.
2
Click once on an item to select it — the slot turns green and asks you to confirm.
3
Click again to confirm the purchase. Coins are deducted and the item goes straight to your inventory.
// your listings menu
✦
ACTIVE — Double-click to cancel and get your item back instantly.
The 10 Coin fee is not refunded on cancel.
✦
EXPIRED — Double-click to return the item to your inventory.
✦
SOLD — Double-click to claim the coins from your sale.
04
// daily draw
Lottery
🎟️
Ticket Price
150 Coins per ticket. You can buy up to 3 tickets per draw — more tickets means a higher chance of winning.
🕕
Draw Time
Every day at 18:00 a winner is randomly drawn from all ticket holders. One ticket = one entry.
💰
The Pot
The pot is the total of all tickets sold: 150 × number of tickets. The winner takes everything.
✅
Offline wins count! If you win while offline, the coins are automatically added to your balance. You'll see the updated amount next time you log in.
CommandDescription
/lottery buyBuy one ticket for 150 Coins — repeat up to 3 times
/lottery infoShows current pot, your ticket count, and time until the next draw

06
// player bounties
Bounty
🎯
Placing a Bounty
Use /bounty <player> <coins>. The coins are deducted from your balance immediately and cannot be refunded.
📈
Stacking
Multiple players can add to the same bounty. The amounts stack — making the target worth more and more.
💀
Claiming
Kill the target to claim their bounty. Coins are awarded immediately and a server broadcast is sent.
CommandDescription
/bounty <player> <amount>Place a bounty — coins deducted immediately, can't be removed
/bounty listView all active bounties sorted by amount (highest first)
