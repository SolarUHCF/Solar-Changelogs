How it Works
1
Type /wager <player> <amount> to challenge someone. They have 60 seconds to accept.
Coins are not deducted until the challenge is accepted.
2
The opponent types /wager accept <yourname>. Coins are deducted from both players and you get teleported to a free arena.
3
A 3-2-1-FIGHT countdown plays. Normal PvP begins — bring your gear, items drop on death as usual.
4
First player to die loses. The winner receives the full pot (2× the wager amount).
Both players get Resistance for 5 seconds after the fight ends. Winner is teleported back after 30 seconds, loser immediately on respawn.

Rules
⚠️
Disconnect = Loss. If you leave the server during a wager your opponent automatically wins the full pot. No exceptions.
⚔️
Items drop normally. This is real PvP — your gear is at risk. The coin wager is separate from your inventory.
📋 Quick Rules
NOTEBoth players must have enough coins before the challenge can be accepted
NOTEYou can only be in one wager at a time — finish your current match first
NOTEIf all 5 arenas are in use the challenge will be rejected — try again shortly
WARNDisconnecting mid-fight counts as an automatic loss — your opponent wins everything
