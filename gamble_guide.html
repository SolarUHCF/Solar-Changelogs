// v3.0 · Major Update
Full Rework
+2 games · balance overhaul · gui fixes
🗑️ Removed
RMVCoin Flip has been removed from the game mode selection
🎮 New Game Modes
NEWCrash — the multiplier rises from 1x upward. Cash out before it crashes or lose everything. The crash point is random every round — it can hit at any time
NEWPlinko — a ball drops through 5 rows of pins and lands in one of 9 slots, each with a different multiplier. Higher risk = rarer slots
💰 Bet System
UPDMinimum bet lowered from 50 to 40 Coins
NEWMaximum bet cap introduced — 200 Coins across all games. All-In is also capped at 200
UPDBet options changed to 40 · 80 · 150 · 200 · All-In
🎰 Slot Machine — Changes
UPD3 of a kind payout raised from 3x to 8x
UPD2 of a kind payout changed — now pays 1.5x (a small profit instead of just a refund)
🎲 Dice Duel — Changes
UPDThe dealer now rolls 2 dice and keeps the higher result — making it harder to beat
UPDWin payout adjusted to 1.9x your bet (was 2x). Tie still refunds your bet
NOTEYour odds of winning are now roughly 1 in 4 instead of 1 in 2
📈 Higher or Lower — Changes
UPDGuessing the same number as the current one now counts as a loss — no more free correct guesses on ties
UPDCash Out is now only available from round 3 onwards — you must commit to at least 3 correct guesses first
UPDMultipliers reworked — see table below

📈 Crash — How It Works
NEWThe multiplier starts at 1x and climbs. Click Cash Out at any point to lock in your current multiplier
NOTEThe crash point is different every round and is not shown to you — it can crash immediately at 1.0x or go all the way to 10x
NOTEThere is a 25% chance it crashes before you can even react (below 1.15x). Cash out early if you want to play it safe
🎯 Plinko — How It Works
NEWA ball drops through 5 rows of pins and bounces left or right randomly at each row
NEWIt lands in one of 9 slots — the center slot pays 0x (full loss), outer slots pay up to 5x
NOTEThe ball is most likely to land near the center — high multiplier slots are rare
