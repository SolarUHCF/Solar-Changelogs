⚡ Overview
⚔️ Kinetic Sword — Energy System
The Kinetic Sword is a new Partner Item that rewards aggressive play. Every hit you land charges your Energy — the higher your energy, the more damage you deal. Energy decays when you stop fighting, so keeping pressure is key.
Base Item
Diamond Sword
Enchants
Sharp III · Unbreaking X
Uses

Energy Range
0 – 100
📖 How It Works
GAIN Hit enemies with the Kinetic Sword to charge Energy. Critical hits give more energy than normal hits.
DECAY Energy slowly drains when you stop hitting. The higher your energy, the faster it decays — so you can't just sit on 90+ passively.
SWEET SPOT In an active 1v1 or teamfight you'll naturally hold around 55–68 Energy. Going higher requires constant aggression.
DEATH Energy resets to 0 on death. The scoreboard line disappears when you have no energy.
