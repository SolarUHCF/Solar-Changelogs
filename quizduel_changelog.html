How it Works
1
Challenge someone with /quizduel challenge <player> <amount>.
They have 60 seconds to accept. Coins are not deducted yet.
2
Once accepted, coins are locked from both players and the match begins immediately.
You will each see a private match start message — nobody else sees your answers.
3
10 questions are asked one by one. Each question appears only in your chat — your opponent sees their own copy. Type the answer directly into chat.
Your answer is hidden from all other players on the server.
4
Whoever answers correctly first gets the point. You have 10 seconds per question — if nobody answers in time, no point is awarded.
Wrong answers don't skip the question — keep trying until time runs out.
5
After each question both players see the current score. The player with the most points after 10 questions wins 2× the wager.
If it's a draw — both players get their coins back.

Rules
💬
Answers are private. Type your answer directly in chat — only you and your opponent are in the duel. Nobody else on the server sees your messages.
⏱️
10 seconds per question. If neither player answers in time, no point is awarded and the next question starts automatically.
🤝
Draw = refund. If both players end with the same score, the wager is cancelled and both players get their coins back.
⚠️
Disconnect = Loss. Leaving the server during a duel counts as an automatic loss — your opponent wins the full pot.
